Contact Details

Get in touch by email

The PICYCLE scheme is based at the School of Computing Science, University of Glasgow. Please mail jeremy.singer@glasgow.ac.uk if you want to get in touch with us.

Send us a parcel from the UK

Thanks for your generosity. If your donated devices will fit into a single parcel weighing less than 1kg then please post the parcel to FREEPOST PICYCLE. For larger consignments, please email us directly to arrange a pickup.

Send us a parcel from outside the UK

We welcome international donors. However you will have to pay international postage rates, which could be expensive. Please address your parcel to:

PICYCLE
University of Glasgow
G12 8QQ
UK
and post via your local mail service.
